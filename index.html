Selwyn Filipino Basketball Club Inc.

Building community through sport

Our Mission

The Club is established and maintained exclusively for amateur sporting and community development purposes.

Our Objectives

  • Promote and support the participation of Filipinos, Kiwis, and migrants in amateur basketball within the Selwyn region.
  • Provide accessible basketball opportunities that improve physical, mental, and social well-being.
  • Encourage health, friendship, teamwork, and inclusion through basketball.
